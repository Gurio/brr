## State maintenance

After completing the task (or making meaningful progress), update
`agent_state.md`:

- Rewrite **Current Focus** to reflect where things stand now.
- Update **Conversation Topics** — add this interaction as a compacted
  entry (one or two lines: what was asked, what was done).  Keep only
  the most recent topics; drop anything older than ~10 entries.
- Update **Decisions**, **Discoveries**, **Next Steps** and **Open
  Questions** as needed.  Remove stale items rather than accumulating.

## Guardrails

Before and during multi-step work, check yourself:

- **Dead ends.** If you have attempted the same approach twice without
  progress, stop and report what you tried rather than retrying.
- **Scope drift.** If the work is expanding beyond the original task,
  pause and note what additional work you identified.  Do not silently
  take on unbounded scope.
- **Circular reasoning.** If your next step is something you already
  did, re-evaluate the approach.
- **Proportionality.** The effort should match the task.  A one-line
  fix does not need a multi-file refactor.  A research question does
  not need a prototype.

When in doubt, write down what you know and what you are unsure about,
and let the user decide the next move.
